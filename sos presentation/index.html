The "Why" Behind the Numbers

It's Not by Accident, It's by Design

Your current digital state is a direct result of a website designed for visuals, not for search engines. It's a common issue, and it's entirely fixable.

  • Content Gaps: The site has minimal text. Search engines have very little content to read and understand what you do, which hurts your ability to rank for valuable terms.
  • Missed SEO Opportunities: Elements like rich image descriptions (alt tags), detailed project case studies as web pages (not just PDFs), and a strategic blog are missing pieces of the puzzle.
  • Low Domain Authority: Without valuable content to attract links from other reputable sites, your site's authority remains low, making it harder to outrank established competitors.
